About the Festival

The Fort Wayne Greek Festival has been a beloved community tradition for over 45 years, organized by Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Church. This four-day celebration brings together thousands of guests to experience authentic Greek culture, cuisine, music, and tradition.

The festival showcases the vibrant Greek heritage of our community, featuring traditional foods prepared fresh daily, live Greek music performances, and captivating traditional Greek dancing. Whether you're a longtime fan or visiting for the first time, the Greek Festival offers something for everyone to enjoy.

Live Entertainment

Enjoy authentic Greek music and traditional dancing throughout the festival

Traditional Greek Dancing

Watch skilled dancers perform traditional Greek dances throughout the day. The most famous is the Kalamatiano, also known as "the twelve-step," which can be danced at various paces and to multiple Greek songs.

Performances happen at 12:30 PM, 6:00 PM, and 8:30 PM daily

Live Greek Music

Experience the sounds of Greece with live music performances featuring traditional Greek instruments and contemporary Greek songs. The festive atmosphere and lively music create the perfect backdrop for celebrating Greek culture.

Cultural Celebration

Beyond food and dancing, the festival celebrates the rich heritage and traditions of the Greek community. Learn about Greek customs, meet local Greek families, and experience the warmth and hospitality that defines Greek culture.
